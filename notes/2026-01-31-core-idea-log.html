D. Reading Rules

  • Same-object rule: all bounds refer to the same fixed observable and the same energy scale.
  • What counts as “uniform”: constants do not depend on $L$ (and typically not on $U$ or $\beta$ unless stated).
  • What the filters do: they restrict the support set $\Lambda$; they do not change the normalization.
