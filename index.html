Independent Research Program
Mathematical Structures and Foundations

Current Research Focus

* Admissibility-based prime readout: Build an operator that predicts the next prime by eliminating non-admissible candidates rather than generating values. * Log–phase landscape reconstruction: Reconstruct the “prime landscape” on the log axis and extract stable spectral/phase invariants. * Closure program: Link structural filtering, readout dynamics, and mean-square consistency constraints into a single, testable framework.

Research Principle

Structure precedes explanation.
Selection precedes generation.
